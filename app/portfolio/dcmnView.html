Overview

DCMN is a marketing and advertising company working for digital brands and startups. After a recent rebranding, the agency needed a new design for the homepage, which was the task assigned to a few of my colleagues and me.

Purpose and context

At the time when our team started working on the redesign of the homepage, DCMN already had a well-developed branding in place. Our task was to tie the visual language of the web design with already existing brand assets. What was even more important, was to tell a coherent story of the brand and available services to its clients.

To understand better our purpose we started running the interviews and created the storytelling around: Media Planning & Booking, Brand Strategy, Creation & Production, Technology & Innovation and Insights.

Objectives

1
Interviewing stakeholders
Talking to owners and teams to understand the expectations and needs of their clients.
2
Understanding the context
Researching the strengths and weaknesses of other agencies working in the same field.
3
Testing and implementing
Running user tests and implementing the changes accordingly to received feedback.

Learnings and takeaways

Seemingly easy project can turn into an enormous task when there are many voices to represent. Filtering through those voices and giving them the same tone was probably the biggest challenge of this assignment. Not to mention understanding different structures and services at the company. Learning the ‘bigger picture’ and later testing the designs was probably the most interesting part of the project.
