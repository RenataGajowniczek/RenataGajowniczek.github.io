Overview

A concept for a learning platform, where people can watch different tutorials related with crafting such as: drawing, painting, sewing, knitting, quilting or making jewelry. Users can not only learn the new skills but also share their progress with other members.

Software
Sketch, Invision
Illustrator
Team

Duration

Purpose and context

The main purpose of Craftsy is to create a community of people interested in learning how to make handmade decorative articles. Users have an opportunity to learn the new skills from recorded video tutorials and share their new knowledge with other members.

My objective was to create tutorials divided on 8 main groups (drawing, painting, etc.). Giving the users an option to browse courses based on creators and possibility to bookmark both projects and artists. Chance to publish executed projects and get feedback from instructors and other users.

Objectives

1
Competitor analysis
Researching strengths and weaknesses based on popular platforms such as CreativeBug and Skillshare.
2
Building hierarchy
Understanding the most important functionalities of the design and making them stand out.
3
Evoking emotion
Bringing vivid colours, organic shapes and textures to connect the branding with creativity and crafting.

Moodboard

I concentrated my research on activities that require crafting and manual skills. Most of my visual inspirations were related with layers, paper textures and organic shapes. I wanted to evoke the feelings that would connect the brand with creativity, playfulness and fun way of learning.

Branding

The visual language of Craftsy is based on contrast between rounded shapes of backgrounds & UI elements and sharpness of logo and typography.

Learnings and takeaways

My final conclusion is that this project was a great lesson in taking compromises between usability and visual language of the product. Few times I had to simplify some of the layouts. Although they were visually interesting their purpose was not obvious for the users. Instead, I had to find the right balance between visual attractiveness and functionality.
